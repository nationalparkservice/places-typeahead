Places Typeahead

Start typing to search for points of interest in the National Park Service's Places system.

Note: This is a prototype and the datasets used should be considered inaccurate and incomplete. We are actively improving the quality and coverage of Places. Improvements made are reflected in near real-time here.

No filters applied.

Usage

Load NPS Bootstrap, jQuery, and the CSS/JavaScript for the plugin:

<link href="http://www.nps.gov/lib/bootstrap/3.3.2/css/nps-bootstrap.min.css" rel="stylesheet">
<link href="http://www.nps.gov/lib/bootstrap/plugins/places-typeahead/latest/typeahead.min.css" rel="stylesheet">
<script src="https://ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jquery/2.1.3/jquery.min.js"></script>
<script src="http://www.nps.gov/lib/bootstrap/plugins/places-typeahead/latest/typeahead.bundle.min.js"></script>
